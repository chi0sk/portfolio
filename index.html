About Me

Early Beginnings (2020)

Skill Development (2021-2022)

Dedicated countless hours to mastering Lua scripting, studying advanced programming concepts, and learning from the developer community. Focused heavily on clean, readable code practices.

Professional Work (2023)

Began taking on professional commissions and collaborating with established development teams. Gained experience in project management, client communication, and deadline management.

Current Focus (2024-Present)

Now 15 years old with 4+ years of development experience and 3 years of professional work. Specializing in complex scripting systems while expanding into UI/UX design and advanced game mechanics. Open to learning VFX, NPC systems, and other emerging technologies.

Terms of Service

Custom Work Only

All scripts and systems I create are custom-made for each client. I don't sell pre-made assets or templates. Every project is tailored to your specific needs and requirements.

Usage Rights & Restrictions

You receive full usage rights for the delivered work within your project. However, you may not:

  • Resell or redistribute the code/assets to third parties
  • Claim the work as your own creation
  • Use the work in ways that violate Roblox Terms of Service

Payment & Refunds

Payment is typically required 50% upfront for larger projects, with the remainder due upon completion. For smaller projects, payment may be required in full upfront.

Refund Policy: No refunds are provided after delivery unless there are critical bugs that prevent the code from functioning as specified. Minor adjustments and revisions are included within the agreed scope.

Currency: All payments must be made in GBP (£).

Turnaround Times

Expected delivery times vary by project complexity:

  • Quick Scripts: 1-3 days
  • UI Systems: 3-7 days
  • Complex Systems: 1-3 weeks
  • Full Game Development: 3+ weeks

Specific timelines will be agreed upon before starting any project.

Communication & Updates

I provide regular updates throughout the development process and am available for questions via Discord. I aim to respond to messages within 24 hours during weekdays.

Revisions & Changes

Minor revisions are included in the project scope. Major changes or additions that significantly alter the original requirements may incur additional charges, which will be discussed before implementation.
